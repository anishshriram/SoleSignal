SoleSignal
A smart shoe insert that detects emergency gestures and automatically alerts your contacts — no hands required.
BLE Sensor CNN on-device Textbelt SMS React Native
01
The Problem
Emergencies don't wait for
you to reach your phone
  • Falls and medical events often leave the person unable to reach their phone
  • Existing solutions (panic buttons, wearables) require deliberate button presses on a device
  • SoleSignal is built into your shoe — activated by a foot gesture, always accessible
02
How It Works
From tap to alert
in seconds

Hardware

FSR 402 pressure sensors embedded in the insole. nRF52840 microcontroller reads A0 + A2 at 20Hz and runs a 1D CNN to classify the tap gesture.

Software

Confirmed gesture → BLE notification → iOS app → GPS location → POST /alerts → Textbelt SMS to emergency contact within seconds.

03
Machine Learning
On-device CNN
— no cloud required
  • 1D Convolutional Neural Network trained on 2-channel pressure time-series (A0 + A2)
  • Sliding window inference — 50 samples at 20Hz = 2.5 seconds of context per decision
  • 3-vote majority — three consecutive confident windows required to trigger, preventing false alarms
  • TFLite model runs entirely on the nRF52840 — under 100KB, no internet needed
